American Swiss

American Swiss first opened its doors in Cape Town in 1896. Now, more than a 100 years later, they are South Africa's leading jeweller, with stores located countrywide. Throughout their history, American Swiss has been at the cutting edge of jewellery trends, complementing the look of the day with the luxury and brilliance of fine quality diamond jewellery and watches.
