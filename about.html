About Pine City Mall

Our bright and spacious centre boasts a secure and homely atmosphere. Perfectly suited to families, we provide both locals and holidaymakers with a relaxed environment where they can enjoy some of the best shopping the Pine City has to offer.

Our fresh assortment of reputable national retailers have been handpicked to suit our community's every need. These stores range from fashion, health and beauty, to gaming and tech, sporting goods and more.

The mall offers a high standard of shopping and a variety of activities for convenience and pleasure, ensuring the satisfaction of our diverse shopper base.

Accessibility

Pine City Mall's extensive parking lot means there is ample parking available. Disabled parking and allocated parking for pregnant women is also provided and we offer wheelchairs should shoppers require assistance. We also have family and disabled bathrooms.

Bring the Kids!

We have a kiddies play area and a giant chess set to keep them entertained while you take a well-deserved break.

Continuous Development

Pine City Mall, which comprises a great part of the Pine City Improvement District, is ideally situated in the fastest development node of the capital. The centre draws people from across the province as well as shoppers from neighbouring countries.

Ownership

This +-22 000m² shopping mall is developed and owned by a consortium comprising PC Properties (Pty) Ltd and it is managed by PC Real Estate (Pty) Ltd.

Security & Parking

We offer an all-in-one shopping experience in a secure environment with parking spaces for 2400 vehicles. The open air parking facilities are free to all shoppers.
